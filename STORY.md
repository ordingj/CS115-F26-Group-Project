# Title: “Final Exam: Room 314

Your final exam starts in 10 minutes.
The problem: you can't find the classroom.

The building looks the same from the outside as it always does. There is construction where your class usually is, but a detour sign points down a hallway you never noticed before.

Your teacher's voice echoes in your head: “The final will be in Room 314. You really don't want to be late.”

⸻

Core Mechanics

- Movement: Forward / Back / Left / Right
- Actions: Check watch, read <>, open <>, knock <>, ???
- Locations: Lobby, hallway, bathroom, intersections, intermediate rooms, ???
- Characters: Janitor, ???
- Time: Time counts down from the start of the game.
- Goal: Find the correct room before time hits 0; if it hits 0 before you find the room, Game Over.

⸻

Puzzle: “Follow the Pattern, Not the Hallway”

The player starts at the foyer; there are two hallways, one is blocked by construction, the other leads past a couple of rooms with strange signs/noises/etc.

After a few movements, the player reaches a 4-way intersection.

The 4-way intersection 'resets' the puzzle sequence and gives the player a clue for the next steps.

⸻

Step 1: Starting Intersection (First Direction + Count)

Environment

Each time player enters the 4-way, they get one of the following clues:

- Flickering light in one hallway - go the opposite way
- Sign for "CS Club Bake Sale" with arrow - go that way
- See a shadow disappearing around a corner at the end of one hallway - go the way they turned

Player passes 3 random weird rooms, arrives at a 3-way - bathroom on one wall, other three directions are hallways.

⸻

Step 2: Bathroom (Second Clue)

Inside:

- Sink runs until you put your soapy hands under it - come up with a puzzle to get water to rinse hands there is a time delay between hands under > off and hands out > on - if player goes under > out > under before the water turns off the water stays on and they rinse their hands and get a hint that they noticed something in the mirror
- Stalls are all locked
- Must wash hands to get next clue - hint to 'look at mirror' and see writing on wall behind you that was written backwards - only notice if you washed hands and get hint (leaning over the sink as you finish rinsing you notice something on the wall behind you above the door')

Clue says "GO LEFT/RIGHT"

⸻

Step 3: Hallway Shift → Janitor Encounter

After going the correct direction once, hallway subtly changes...

Janitor is mopping the floor.

⸻

Step 4: Janitor (Third Clue)

Janitor has headphones on and is humming a tune. He doesn't notice/ignores the player no matter what they do.

Player needs to listen to the tune and will 'remember' the lyrics but not the name. The song title is the clue for the next direction. Make a list of 10 popular songs with the word "left" and 10 popular songs with the word "right" in the title. Have the player 'remember' the chorus. Randomly select one of those songs to be the janitor's tune each time the player encounters him.

⸻

Step 5: Room 314

If they go the right way, do another couple of random rooms, then they arrive at Room 314. They can enter.

⸻

The Wrong Way:

If at any point the player goes the wrong way, give them a few random rooms and then back at the 4-way with new clues. Reset the puzzles/randomize the clues.

1. Wrong Sequence

- You end up back at start:
  - something ominous like "You think you've been here before."

1. Subtle Tracking

As the time counts down, start adding ominous messages like "You are standing underneath a vent that is blasting cold air." "You hear footsteps behind you, but when you turn around, no one is there." "You feel like you're being watched." "You hear a whisper, but can't make out the words."

⸻

Why This Works

- Combines movement puzzle + environmental storytelling
- Forces player to:
  - observe patterns
  - interact (wash hands, observe)
  - remember sequence
- Feels like:
  _solving something logical
  _ but inside something irrational
  ⸻

Ending Variants

Good Ending:
You find Room 314. Inside: normal classroom.
Professor: “You have 4 hours to complete the exam...”

Bad Ending:
Time runs out.
The hallway goes dark. You are eaten by a grue.

Weird Ending (humor):
Arrive in classroom with at least 5 minutes remaining.
Note written on: “Final moved online. Check Canvas.”
